### 4. Deep-dive: one at-risk dish
The **Tagine d'agneau aux pruneaux (Grand)** is our most significant margin risk. The ERP system relies on stale cost data, leading to a misleading view of its profitability.

| Metric | Reported | Effective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Total Cost | MAD 88.60 | MAD 94.60 |
| Margin | MAD 21.40 | MAD 15.40 |
| Margin % | 19.5% | 14.0% |

The primary driver is the 15.4% cost creep in lamb. At a 14% effective margin, this dish is barely contributing to overhead.

### 5. Recommended actions
* **Reprice Tagine d'agneau**: Increase the price of the "Grand" Tagine d'agneau to MAD 125 to restore the margin to a healthy level, given the current lamb cost volatility.
* **Renegotiate Lamb Procurement**: The 15.4% cost increase for lamb is unsustainable. Initiate immediate discussions with suppliers to lock in better rates or source alternative vendors.
* **Promote High-Margin Sides**: Given the low margins on premium tagines, train staff to upsell the **Harira** and **Salade marocaine** as starters; these items carry >84% margins and will help offset the lower-margin main courses.
* **Update ERP Costs**: Manually update the ingredient cost for lamb in the ERP to reflect the current MAD 98.13/kg to ensure future reporting is accurate.
